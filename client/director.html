Director's Pen

I am the director of this attractive School, where each child is accepted and appreciated for his/her uniqueness. My teachers show one-on-one attention to make your child feel comfortable and be successful. We are trying to build a warm, welcoming and fostering environment to our children.

Let me say few line about education, Education is the most important factor. which plays a great role in the development of an individual as well as country. Now a day, it has become a vital factor for the future brightness of the new generations of any Society. I had a dream to provide good Education to the children who are from poor or under poverty.

Because these common man due to financial crunch they are not able to sent their child in a reputed private school. To see all those issues I had a challenge to bring the good education for child, especially for the family who are financially week. There for I have established, AS-SABEEL PUBLIC SCHOOL, Line bazar, Purnea. In this school we provided best education, lots of facilities in minimum fee.

To full fill this, my teachers and staff help a lots, also I wants to say, the teachers and staff must work as mentors and guides. It help children realize their true potential. Hence this aim I should be confident a co-ordinate effort is being made by the teachers and students of the school to ensure all round and holistic development of every child in the school. I am more than positive that the school will continue its goal of excellence in the coming years.

- Director A.P.S. Purnea
